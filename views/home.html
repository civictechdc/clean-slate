Seal Your Washington, DC Criminal Record

This tool will help check whether you can get your adult criminal records sealed.

You'll need:

  • The date of your conviction
  • The name of the crime you were committed of or charged with
  • We’ll help you figure out the rest!
Check your eligibility now

Sealing your criminal record in DC is a three-step process:

Step #1

Determine if you are eligible to seal your record.

Jump to: Check Eligibility

Step #2

If you're eligible, you'll need your criminal record.

Step #3

Apply to get your record sealed.
